Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee OM KBE FRS FREng FRSA FBCS (born 8 June 1955), also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. Berners-Lee is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) which oversees the continued development of the Web.The Wealth Record has his net worth at $60 million - or more than £45.5 million. According to The Richest, his personal wealth is valued at $50 million while Celebrity Net Worth reckon he is worth $10 million.
